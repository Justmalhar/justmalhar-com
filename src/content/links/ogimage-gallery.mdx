[OGimage.gallery](https://www.ogimage.gallery/) collects standout Open Graph images from companies like Stripe, Vercel, Apple, and Framer. It is a quick way to study how the best teams treat link previews as a design surface.

Why I bookmark it:
- Real OG images from production sites, not mockups
- Tags for SaaS, creative, portfolios, animated OG, and more
- Useful when designing social cards, meta images, or share previews for a new project

If you care about how your links look on Twitter, Slack, or iMessage, this gallery is worth browsing.
