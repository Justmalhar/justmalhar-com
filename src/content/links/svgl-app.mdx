[SVGL](https://svgl.app/) is a clean, searchable library of SVG brand logos. It is faster than hunting down official assets or exporting blurry PNGs when you just need a crisp logo in a sidebar, footer, or case study.

Why it is handy:
- Hundreds of logos with consistent SVG formatting
- Sortable and searchable by name or category
- Easy to copy SVG markup directly into code

I used it while building out the Work page logos — worth keeping around for any UI that needs brand marks.
