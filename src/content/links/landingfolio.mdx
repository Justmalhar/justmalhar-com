[Landingfolio](https://www.landingfolio.com/) is one of my go-to references when I need landing page ideas. It collects real-world examples across SaaS, product, marketing, and dozens of other categories.

What makes it useful:
- High-quality site and section inspiration, not generic Dribbble shots
- A large component library you can copy into Tailwind, Webflow, or Figma projects
- Filters by industry, page type, and color when you need something specific

Great for kicking off a new page or stealing a layout pattern that already works.
